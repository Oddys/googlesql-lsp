Zed ⇄ googlesql extension: registration and low-level interaction

How Zed discovers this repo's zed-extension, compiles and instantiates it as a WebAssembly component, and later calls back into it — every hop across the WASM ABI, not just the LSP-visible surface. Phase 1 is extension load + language-server registration at startup. Phase 2 traces one language_server_command call end to end, including the guest→host callbacks it makes to resolve the googlesql-lsp binary's path and environment. Once Zed has a Command, it spawns the binary and speaks LSP JSON-RPC directly — see editor-lsp-sequence.html for that handshake onward.
